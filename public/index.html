FAQ
Common Questions
Where does the data come from?
ScoutMind uses real season data from official league statistics, cross-referenced with public sources including FBref, Transfermarkt, and ESPN. Data is updated at the start of each season and refreshed throughout the year.
How accurate are the player recommendations?
Recommendations are based on a stat-matching algorithm that compares a player's real performance data against the ideal profile generated from your team's weakness. The match score (0вЂ“100%) reflects how closely a player fits the profile across multiple weighted statistics.
Can I use ScoutMind for lower leagues like SГ©rie C?
Yes. ScoutMind covers SГ©rie A, B, and C in Brazil, plus the top 5 European leagues and MLS. We are actively expanding to more leagues. For leagues not yet covered, clubs can manually input their stats.
Is there a free trial for Club plans?
Club Basic and Club Pro plans are available by contacting us directly. We offer a 14-day free pilot for clubs interested in testing the product with their real team data before committing.
What is the community feature?
Scout Pro subscribers get access to team community pages where fans can discuss transfers, vote in polls, share scouting reports, and debate who their club should sign next вЂ” all powered by real ScoutMind analysis.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. There are no long-term contracts on the Scout Pro plan. Club plans have a 30-day notice period.
